Species Fossils Extinction

Dinosaurs ("Terrible lizards") first appeared between 245 and 233.23 million years ago.
They were the dominant creatures at the time and are quite popular in modern media.
We know of the dinosaurs through fossil evidence (some can be seen in the fossils page!).
Dinosaurs actually still exist today, just not as you'd think. Birds are descendants of the avian dinosaurs!
Some popular dinosaurs include: Tyrannosaurus Rex, Stegosaurus, Triceratops, Pterodactyl, Brachiosaurus, Velociraptors, etc.
